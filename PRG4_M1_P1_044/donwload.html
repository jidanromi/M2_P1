New Agent



DEADLOCK



Norwegian detective Deadlock deploys a series of
advanced nanowires to secure the battlefield
from even the deadliest attacks. No one can escape
his strict supervision, or survive his cruelty.

New Map



SUNSET


Sunset is one of VALORANT's more traditional maps
with two sites and three lanes. Its one additional feature
is a mechanical door found between B Market and Mid Courtyard.
Players can use a switch on the Market side of the door to close or open it.
This door is impenetrable while closed but has 500 HP,
allowing players to damage and eventually destroy it.
Once destroyed, the doorway remains permanently open for the rest of the round.
